List of ingredients (1 serving)

  • Steak - 1 ~ 1.5 inch thick, 10-16 oz.
  • Butter (Unsalted) - 2 tablespoon
  • Thyme (Fresh) - 2 spriggs
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Pepper - 1 tsp
  • Garlic (Whole, peeled) - 3-5 cloves (Unless you are a vampire)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 1 tbs

Time to cook! Please follow this easy step by step guide for a yummy steak

  1. 1. Leave the steak out to be room temperature, pat dry with paper towel (to make a nice crust!)
  2. Lightly coat steak with olive oil
  3. Salt and Pepper on both sides
  4. Heat a heavy pan (preferably cast iron) on high on stove top
  5. When the pan is hot, drizzle some more olive oil onto the pan and wait for it to smoke
  6. Sear Steak for 2-3 minutes on each side, look for a nice brown color, only flip once
  7. put in garlic and thyme next to the steak
  8. drop the temp down to medium
  9. Baste the steak with butter and the herbs
  10. Refer to This Guide for doneness
  11. Let steak rest for 10 minutes
  12. Cut and enjoy!
